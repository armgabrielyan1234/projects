Air Jordan Dunk Blazer

The Nike Blazer is a sneaker manufactured by Nike. It was originally
released in 1973 as a basketball shoe.
The Beaverton, Oregon-based brand named the design “Blazer”
after its regional NBA team, the Portland Trail Blazers.
The original shoe was made out of three main components:
the upper portion was made of leather, the tongue was made
of nylon, and the midsole was made of rubber.
