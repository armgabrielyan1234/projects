Air Jordan is a line of basketball shoes and athletic apparel
produced by American corporation Nike, Inc. The first Air Jordan shoe
was produced for Hall of Fame former basketball player Michael Jordan
during his time with the Chicago Bulls in late 1984 and released to the
public on April 1, 1985. The shoes were designed for Nike
by Peter Moore, Tinker Hatfield, and Bruce Kilgore.
As the popularity of the Air Jordan brand grew throughout
the years, as of 2022 the company brought in over $5.1 billion,
earning Michael Jordan 5% of the revenue, totaling $256 million for
that year.
